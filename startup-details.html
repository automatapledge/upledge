Our Mission

WERI is the online sharing platform dedicated to electric mobility that allows private users and companies to choose and drive an electric car,van or scooter for hours, weeks or days without having to buy it.

The Changemakers

Cosimo Cecchini

As a Marketing and Strategy expert and CEO of WERI, we are combining our competences and experiences with our committment to make our cities a better place to live.

Giuseppe de Candia

Co-founder, IT engineer and CTO, we are combining our competences and experiences with our committment to make our cities a better place to live.

Product / Services / Technology

The platform is composed by the app to book, rent and unlock an EV where needed when needed and a back-office management system for companies and car dealers to better manage their fleet. For both of them, we track the quantity of CO2 saved and we convert in Green Point, necessary to achieve the sustainable goals and the gamification.

Our Impact

We want to create a community of Green Priders, Influencers of Sustainability. For this reason, each booking made, customers monitor: and , through the

  • the grams of CO2 saved
  • the contribution to the achievement of sustainability objectives
  • indirect impact via gamification, such as planting a tree, saving bees etc.
